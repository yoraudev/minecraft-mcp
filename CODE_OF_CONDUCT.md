# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We are committed to a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free community for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

1. Be respectful and constructive.
2. Focus on technical feedback, not personal attacks.
3. Assume good intent and communicate clearly.
4. Help others learn and collaborate.

## Unacceptable Behavior

1. Harassment, hate speech, or discriminatory language.
2. Personal attacks, insults, or intimidation.
3. Deliberate disruption, trolling, or bad-faith engagement.
4. Sharing private or sensitive information without consent.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and related communication channels.

## Reporting

Report unacceptable behavior to project maintainers through private channels when possible.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may take any action they consider appropriate, including warning, content removal, temporary restriction, or permanent ban.
